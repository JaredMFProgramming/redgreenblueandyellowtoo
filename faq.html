Frequently Asked Questions

1. So, this must be another one of these sites like Purple.com.

This definitely is one of these sites, alright. You can even call it "RGBY," which is short for "Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow, Too!"

2. Is there anything I can do?

You can hang around in the glory of the primary colors, one of them used for printing, and one of them for electronic visual displays.

3. Where can I find colors other than red, green, blue, and yellow?

I don't know, but have you tried the Konami Code? I give many thanks to the guys at SnapTortoise for their awesome Konami Code implementation that I put in this website.

4. How did you make this website?

I borrowed some source code from Damon Zuconni's website, "Sometimes Red, Sometimes Blue," VaughnGH's NotPurple.com, and Jeff Abrahamson's internet sensation, and added more stuff, like the colors yellow and green, and additional pages. (Thank you, guys!)

5. I would like to be a part of this website.

You definitely can!

6. Can I buy or lease the website?

I am terribly sorry. This website is not for sale.

7. What games can I play on this website?

The games will be coming soon to this website; I promise.
